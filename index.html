Who We Are

Rooted in Science,
Driven by Purpose

Noxus is a global agro-technology company headquartered across three continents. We develop intelligent systems that empower farmers, agribusinesses, and governments to make data-driven decisions that increase yield, reduce resource waste, and protect natural ecosystems.

Our multidisciplinary team of agronomists, data scientists, engineers, and ecologists work in harmony — creating solutions that are as deeply scientific as they are deeply humane.
